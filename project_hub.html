Nomination Website for YouTube STEM Educator | July 2025 | Web Projects, Community Engagement
🔍 Idea Behind the Project

This project aimed to spotlight freely available STEM education on YouTube and celebrate self-learning. It highlights the power of community-driven knowledge sharing.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
  • ChatGPT: Partnered on site layout, content writing, and idea framing.
  • Firebase: Hosted the nomination form and managed submissions.
  • Airtable: Organized volunteer workflows and nominations.
  • HTML/CSS: Built the main website layout and components.
📋 Responsibilities & Actions
  • Researched relevant STEM awards.
  • Created the website and integrated Firebase form.
  • Designed Airtable project board for team management.
  • Managed timelines and nomination tasks across roles.
🎯 Intended Outcome

The project seeks to bring attention to alternative education paths, high-impact educators and prove the power of structured, grassroots projects.

🧠 Skills Highlighted

Project Management, Firebase Integration, Workflow Design, Initiative, Research
